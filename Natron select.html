Explore Our Destinations

Tanzania is a land of breathtaking beauty and diverse landscapes. From the iconic Serengeti to the serene Lake Natron, our destinations offer something for every traveler. Discover the wonders of Tanzania with Natron Select Safaris.

Serengeti National Park

Known for the Great Migration, the Serengeti offers unparalleled wildlife viewing opportunities. Spot the Big Five and enjoy the vast, open savannah landscapes.

Ngorongoro Crater

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Ngorongoro Crater is home to a dense population of wildlife, including lions, elephants, and the critically endangered black rhino.

Lake Manyara

Famous for its tree-climbing lions and large flocks of flamingos, Lake Manyara National Park offers diverse ecosystems and a perfect destination for a day trip.

Tarangire National Park

Known for its large elephant herds and iconic baobab trees, Tarangire provides a serene and intimate safari experience.

Lake Natron

A unique destination with alkaline waters and vibrant red hues, Lake Natron is a breeding ground for flamingos and a haven for birdwatchers and photographers.

Southern Tourist Circuit

Explore the untouched wilderness of Selous Game Reserve, Ruaha National Park, and Mikumi National Park for a more off-the-beaten-path experience.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in the lives of the Maasai, Hadzabe, and Datoga tribes. Participate in cultural dances and learn about traditional hunting techniques.

Mountain Hiking

Climb Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak, or explore the scenic trails of Mount Meru and the Usambara Mountains for breathtaking views and adventure.
